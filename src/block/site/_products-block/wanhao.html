About product

The Endurance WanHao DuPlicator i3 is a 3D printer + engraving and cutting machine (combo).
 
Endurance upgraded a very popular 3D printer WanHao DuPlicator i3 model with a powerful 2.1W Endurance L-Cheapo laser and turned the 3D printer into an engraving and cutting machine.
 
The Endurance laser engraver is a user-friendly device. It can engrave various surfaces but metallic and ceramic. To make an engraving yesterday you had to approach an advertising agency or a special engraving workshop. Endurance makes engraving more affordable and easy.
 
Cut and grave on:
  • wood
  • acrylic
  • PLA
  • ABS
  • felt
  • fabric
  • paper
  • cardboard
  • hardboard
